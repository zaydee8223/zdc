CRAFTING MY NARRATIVE

Hi, I'm Zaydee Dominguez-Chang, a passionate Full-Stack Developer based in Indianapolis, Indiana. With expertise in Front-End Development, I specialize in crafting clean, minimalist aesthetics for web projects.

My journey into web development began at the age of 11, igniting a lifelong passion for coding and technology. Since then, I've been driven by a zealous dedication to mastering both front-end and back-end development.

As a First-Generation Latina woman, I'm committed to paving the way for others in my community and serving as a role model for aspiring developers. I'm deeply passionate about advocating for diversity and inclusion in the tech industry.

In my free time, I enjoy traveling, and exploring local spots, experimenting with photography, going to concerts, and discovering new music.

Let's connect and collaborate on something great!
